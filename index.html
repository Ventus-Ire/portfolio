Shader and VFX Library

Samples of different shaders and visual effects I made over the years.

Software Developer

I developed shaders and visual effects using URP in Unity. Click here for a video of some of the assets I made for UI, interactions, and visual indicators.

Ronin Tower

A samurai tower defense game made with Unity and written in C#

Gameplay Dev/Level and Effects Dev

I developed a reusable ability system, gameplay mechanics and a health system for the main tower, and an enemy attack system. I also designed the level using Unity store assets. I learned to work with new tools in Unity and collaborate with a small team in a fast paced environment.

Unreal VR

A series of VR simulations made with Unreal Engine 4 and Blueprint

Sole Developer

I developed a knife that can cut through meshes, virtual headphones using sound attenuation, and worked with physics materials to simulate stone skipping on water. I developed these interactions as my intro into the Unreal Engine and the blueprint system.

Vrm

A VR simulation of a cadaver lab made with Unity and written in C#

Lead Developer for VR Interactions

As one of the lead developers, I improved outdated VR Arm interactions and developed a diegetic UI for displaying arm information. I learned to lead a team of developers, keep records of progress, and work on my own contributions at the same time.

The Otherside

An 3D isometric puzzle game made with Unity and written in C#

Gameplay Dev/Level and Effects Dev

Working with a team of four, I developed a block system to create the obstacles in the puzzles and a system of switches that could move the obstacles. I also designed levels that could be solved multiple ways.

Audio Visualizer

An audio visualizer built from the ground up using HTML, Canvas, and JS

Sole Developer

Being the sole developer, I made everything from the canvas drawings, to the particle systems, to the control system to change the look of the visualizer. The songs were borrowed from the YouTuber "Maritumix" and the show "K". (Chrome Only)
